Brief History

It was produced by a combination of three first-year students and one second-year student at Myongji College. It consisted of 1 front-end, 1 back-end, 1 image designer, and 1 roulette designer.

My Career

As a front creator, I studied front web development for about 4 months and was in charge of web page design production.
